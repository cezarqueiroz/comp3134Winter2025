1 - A server port is a number used to identify specific services on a server.

2 - The port range is from 0 to 65535, split into:

0-1023: Well-known ports

1024-49151: Registered ports

49152-65535: Dynamic/private ports

3 - 16 bits, allowing values from 0 to 65535.
